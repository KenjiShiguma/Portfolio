Gamma Stryker 5™

Online Multiplayer Spaceship Combat Game

Gamma Stryker 5™ was a 3D spaceship combat game for mobile, where players battle with spaceships. Four players compete against each other via online matchmaking, to eliminate each other and be the last player alive. Players thrust and dash at high speeds through a dangerous asteroid belt, barrel roll dodge incoming threats, and obliterate their opponents with laser projectiles. Players customize their ships with different components to optimize their playstyle and alter the aesthetics.

This mobile game featured touch screen and gyroscope controls, 3D graphics and physics, and online multiplayer gameplay. The footage shown is one of the prototypes that used placeholder art assets to test basic gameplay mechanics such as movement, combat, auto-targeting, etc. Kermit developed several prototypes to chiefly test specific mechanics such as the ship customization system, the online matchmaking system, and in-app purchases (IAP).

Kermit was the lead programmer, and had artists working with him on the release build. The game was supposed to launch initially on the Google Play Store, and then eventually on the Apple App Store, however, development was cancelled and the project was never finished.
